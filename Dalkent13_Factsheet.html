Investment Philosophy

Managed using a systematic valuation framework with full skin in the game — the same approach applied to professional strategy work. Every position is backed by a three-model blend calibrated to sector characteristics.

Valuation: DCF, DDM & EPV across 100+ FTSE stocks. Bought at a discount to intrinsic value, sold at fair value — never on momentum.
Signals: Entry at Value Ratio ≥1.25 (>25% below target). Strong Buy ≥1.50. Exit ≤1.00.
Positioning: Max 5% per position. Profits locked in systematically. Assumptions: Risk-free 4.9% | ERP 5.0% | Terminal growth 2.5%.
Track record: +129.9% since Aug 2022 vs +72.1% S&P 500 and +45.8% FTSE 100. Model-driven, fully transparent — published weekly on FTSE Valuation Insights (Substack).
